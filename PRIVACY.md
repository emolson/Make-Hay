# Make Hay Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

Make Hay is a self-control app that helps you temporarily limit access to apps you choose until you reach your daily fitness goals.

## Summary

- Make Hay does not require an account.
- Make Hay does not sell your data.
- Make Hay does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or a backend service.
- Make Hay processes your data on-device.

## Information Make Hay Accesses

### Health Data

If you grant permission, Make Hay reads selected Apple Health data so it can evaluate whether you have met your configured goals.

This may include:

- Steps
- Active energy
- Exercise minutes
- Workout data

Make Hay requests read access only. It does not write data to Apple Health.

### Screen Time / Family Controls Data

If you grant permission, Make Hay uses Apple's Family Controls and Managed Settings frameworks to apply shields to the apps and categories you choose.

This involves locally storing your selected app-blocking configuration so the app and its extension can stay in sync on your device.

### App Configuration Data

Make Hay stores the minimum local configuration needed to function, such as:

- Your selected goals
- Your blocked app selections
- Onboarding and permission state
- Scheduling and temporary unlock state needed for app behavior

## How Your Data Is Used

Make Hay uses this information only to:

- Evaluate whether you have reached your goal
- Update blocked or unblocked app state on your device
- Restore your settings between launches
- Show you current permission and sync status

## Data Sharing

Make Hay does not send your personal data to a remote server.

Make Hay does not share your Health data, app selections, or usage configuration with advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.

## Data Storage and Retention

Your configuration is stored locally on your device, including app-group storage used to coordinate with the Device Activity extension.

Health data used by Make Hay is read from Apple Health when needed. Make Hay does not maintain a cloud copy of that data.

You can stop future access at any time by:

- Revoking Health access in the Health app
- Revoking Screen Time / Family Controls access in iOS settings
- Deleting the app from your device

## Third-Party Services

Make Hay relies on Apple platform services and frameworks, including Apple Health, Family Controls, Managed Settings, Device Activity, and background task APIs.

Make Hay does not include third-party analytics, crash reporting, advertising, or social SDKs.

## Children's Privacy

Make Hay is designed as a self-control tool for the person using the device. It is not marketed as a child-directed service.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app's data practices change. Material changes will be reflected on this page by updating the date above.
